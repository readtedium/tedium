Today in Tedium: Of the many fascinating experiments on the internet these days involving red hot nickel balls, my personal favorite is this one, involving two guitars. Then Neuland used the nickel ball to destroy the strings. It’s far from the first example of someone messing with guitar strings just to see what might happen—in fact, there’s something of a whole subgenre of guitar playing based on the concept of modifying or breaking guitars in unusual ways.
