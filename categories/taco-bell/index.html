Floating Fast Food

After I write this piece, I may be forced to apologize again. That’s because I’m about to bring up a bad memory for the fast-food giant. In the late ‘80s, the World’s Fair was still a fairly Big Deal in North America, and Vancouver played host to one of the most notable World Expo galas, Expo ’86.
